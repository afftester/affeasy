## 1. Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- An active [Rakuten Advertising Publisher Account](https://rakutenadvertising.com/)
- An AffEasy account

<Info>
  If you don't have a Rakuten publisher account, [sign up
  here](https://auth.rakutenmarketing.com/auth/realms/rakuten-advertising/protocol/openid-connect/registrations?client_id=publisher-dashboard&redirect_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fpublisher.rakutenadvertising.com&response_type=code)
</Info>

## 2. Obtain Your Site ID (SID)

- Log in to your Rakuten publisher account.
- On the dashboard, locate your Site ID (SID).
- Copy the numeric portion only (e.g., for SID4022211, copy 4022211).

<Info>
  
  AffEasy currently supports one SID per account. We're working on supporting multiple SIDs in the future.

</Info>

## 3. Access the Developer Portal

- Click the menu icon in the top right corner of your publisher dashboard.
- Select "Developer Portal" to access the [Rakuten Developer Portal](https://developers.rakutenadvertising.com/)
- You should be automatically logged in. If not, use your publisher account credentials.

## 5. Connect Rakuten to AffEasy

- Go to your [AffEasy account settings.](https://app.affeasy.link/settings/networks)
- Click "Add".

<Frame>
  <img
    className="rounded-lg border border-gray-100"
    src="images/affiliate-networks/affeasy-network.png"
    alt="Rakuten Dashboard"
    width="1200"
    height="630"
  />
</Frame>

- Select Rakuten from the list.
- Enter the following details:

  - Account ID (SID from Step 2)
  - Client ID (from Step 4)
  - Client Secret (from Step 4)

- Click "Add Network" to finalize the integration.

  />
</Frame>

## What's Next

Congratulations! Your Rakuten Advertising account is now connected to AffEasy. Here's what you can expect:

- AffEasy will automatically sync your Rakuten brand partnerships.
- You can now create affiliate links for your Rakuten brands directly from your AffEasy dashboard.
- No need to use Rakuten's tools for link generation anymore.

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues during the integration process:

- Double-check all entered information for accuracy.
- Ensure your Rakuten account is in good standing.
- Clear your browser cache and try again.
- Contact AffEasy support if problems persist.

We're constantly improving our integration process. If you have any feedback or suggestions, please let us know!
