## 1. Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- An active Amazon Associates Account
- An [AffEasy account](https://app.affeasy.link)

<Tip>
  If you don't have an Amazon Associates account, [sign up
  here](https://affiliate-program.amazon.com/signup)
</Tip>

## 2. Obtain Your Amazon Store ID

- Log in to your Amazon Associates account.
- From the settings dropdown, select [Manage Your Tracking IDs](https://affiliate-program.amazon.com/home/account/tag/manage)

<Frame>
  <img
    className="rounded-lg border border-gray-100"
    src="/images/affiliate-networks/amazon/amazon-settings.png"
    alt="Amazon Tag Settings Menu"
    width="1200"
    height="630"
  />
</Frame>

- On the Manage Your Tracking IDs page, you'll see a list of your tracking IDs (also known as Store IDs or tags). Locate the tracking ID that you want to conenct to AffEasy
  Your tracking ID is the alphanumeric code (e.g. store-20).

<Frame>
  <img
    className="rounded-lg border border-gray-100"
    src="/images/affiliate-networks/amazon/amazon-tracking-id.png"
    alt="Amazon Store ID"
    width="1200"
    height="630"
  />
</Frame>
- Copy your chosen tracking ID. You'll need this for the next step when connecting
to AffEasy.
<Info>
  AffEasy currently supports one tracking ID per account. We're working on
  supporting multiple tracking IDs in the future.
</Info>

## 3. Connect Amazon to AffEasy

- Go to your [AffEasy account settings.](https://app.affeasy.link/settings/networks)
- Click "Add".

<Frame>
  <img
    className="rounded-lg border border-gray-100"
    src="/images/affiliate-networks/affeasy-network.png"
    alt="AffEasy Add Network"
    width="1200"
    height="630"
  />
</Frame>

- Select "Amazon" from the list.
- Enter your Store ID in the provided field.
- Click "Add Network" to finalize the integration.

<Frame>
  <img
    className="rounded-lg border border-gray-100"
    src="/images/affiliate-networks/amazon/affeasy-addamazon.png"
    alt="AffEasy Add Amazon"
    width="300"
    height="150"
  />
</Frame>

## What's Next

Congratulations! Your Amazon Associates account is now connected to AffEasy. Here's what you can expect:

You can now create affiliate links for Amazon products directly from your AffEasy dashboard.
AffEasy will automatically use your Store ID when generating Amazon affiliate links.

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues during the integration process:

- Double-check that you've entered your Store ID correctly.
- Ensure your Amazon Associates account is in good standing.
- Clear your browser cache and try again.
- Contact AffEasy support if problems persist.

We're constantly improving our integration process. If you have any feedback or suggestions, please let us know!
