# AffEasy Business Plan

Learn more about the AffEasy Business plan and its features to scale your affiliate marketing operations.

Last updated: September 5, 2024

## Pro Plan Features

AffEasy offers one of the most powerful introductory paid plans in the industry – our **Pro plan**.

With our Pro plan, you can:

- Create up to 1,000 **links** a month with your own **custom domains**
- Update link destinations unlimited times
- Get **in-depth analytics** on your links' performance for up to 50,000 clicks a month

However, as your usage grows, you may want to upgrade to the AffEasy Business Plan to unlock more features and benefits.

## Benefits of AffEasy Business Plan

Subscribing to AffEasy Business unlocks benefits such as more links, more tracked clicks, extended analytics retention period, and more.

## How much does AffEasy Business cost?

AffEasy's basic Business plan is priced at $59 per month – or $49 per month if billed annually.

As you scale your affiliate marketing operations, we also offer additional Business tiers with higher usage limits for links, tracked clicks, and other features.

Here's a quick overview of the different Business tiers:

## How to upgrade to Business?

To upgrade to AffEasy Business, follow these steps:

1. First, **create a workspace** if you haven't already.
2. Navigate to your workspace page.
3. Click on the **Upgrade** button in the top menu bar.

## Business Plan Benefits for Affiliate Marketers

Upgrading to AffEasy Business can significantly enhance your affiliate marketing operations:

1. **Increased Link Capacity**: Create up to 5,000 links per month (or more with higher tiers), perfect for large-scale affiliate campaigns across multiple niches.
2. **Enhanced Analytics**: Track up to 150,000 clicks per month with two years of data retention for comprehensive performance analysis and trend identification.
3. **Extended Team Collaboration**: Add up to 15 team members (or more with higher tiers) to manage complex affiliate marketing operations efficiently.
4. **Advanced Customization**: Utilize up to 40 custom domains for improved branding across various marketing channels.
5. **Scalability**: As your affiliate business grows, easily upgrade to higher Business tiers to accommodate increased traffic and link volume.

By leveraging these advanced features, you can optimize your affiliate marketing strategies, manage multiple campaigns effectively, and potentially increase your revenue significantly.

If you have any questions about the Business plan or need assistance with the upgrade process, please [contact our support team](mailto:support@affeasy.link).
