## Free Plan Features

AffEasy offers one of the most generous free plans in the affiliate link management industry. With our free tier, you can:

- [Create links](/quickstart/guide) with [custom domains](/domains/add-custom-domain)
- Update link destinations unlimited times
- Access **in-depth analytics** on your links' performance (including geolocation, device, browser, and referrer information)

However, as your affiliate marketing efforts grow, you may want to [upgrade to the AffEasy Pro Plan](https://affeasy.link/pricing) to unlock more features and benefits.

## Benefits of the Pro Plan

Subscribing to AffEasy Pro unlocks benefits such as more tracked clicks, [root domain redirect](/features/how-to-redirect-root-domain), [custom QR codes](/features/custom-qr-codes), and more.

Here's how AffEasy Pro compares to the free tier:

| Features                  | Free    | Pro    |
| ------------------------- | ------- | ------ |
| Number of links per month | 25      | 1,000  |
| Tracked clicks per month  | 1,000   | 50,000 |
| Analytics retention       | 30 days | 1 year |
| Number of custom domains  | 3       | 10     |
| Number of Teammates       | 1       | 5      |
| Number of Tags            | 5       | 25     |
| Root domain redirect      | ✘       | ✔     |
| Advanced link features    | ✘       | ✔     |
| API Access                | Limited | ✔     |

You can check out the full pricing comparison on our [pricing page](https://affeasy.link/pricing).

## How much does AffEasy Pro cost?

AffEasy Pro is priced at `$19.99` per month or `$14.99` per month if billed annually.

## How to upgrade to Pro?

To upgrade to AffEasy Pro, follow these steps:

1. First, [**create a workspace**](/workspace/what-is-a-workspace) if you haven't already.
2. Navigate to your workspace page.
3. Click on the **Upgrade** button in the top menu bar.

## Pro Plan Benefits for Affiliate Marketers

Upgrading to AffEasy Pro can significantly enhance your affiliate marketing efforts:

1. **Increased Link Capacity**: Create up to 1,000 links per month, perfect for large-scale campaigns.
2. **Enhanced Analytics**: Track up to 50,000 clicks per month with a full year of data retention for in-depth performance analysis.
3. **Team Collaboration**: Add up to 5 team members to streamline your affiliate marketing operations.
4. **Advanced Customization**: Utilize root domain redirects and custom QR codes for improved branding.
5. **API Access**: Integrate AffEasy seamlessly with your existing marketing tools and workflows.

By leveraging these advanced features, you can optimize your affiliate marketing strategies and potentially increase your revenue.
