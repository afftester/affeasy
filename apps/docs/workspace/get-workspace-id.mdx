## Access Your Workspace

If you haven't created a workspace yet, create a new one.
If you already have a workspace, navigate to it from your AffEasy dashboard.

## Go to Settings

Click on the Settings tab in the navigation bar at the top of your workspace.

## Locate Your Workspace ID

Scroll down the settings page until you find the Workspace ID section.
Your Workspace ID will be displayed here.

## Why You Might Need Your Workspace ID

Your Workspace ID is a unique identifier for your AffEasy workspace. You might need it for:

- Contacting support about workspace-specific issues
- Accessing certain API features

If you have any questions about using your Workspace ID or need assistance, please [contact our support team](mailto:support@affeasy.link)
